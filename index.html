Understanding Orca Solana

Orca Solana is a decentralized exchange (DEX) and liquidity aggregator built on the Solana blockchain. As a DEX, Orca enables users to trade tokens directly from their wallets, providing a non-custodial and permissionless trading experience. The platform also serves as a liquidity aggregator, consolidating liquidity from multiple sources to offer users the best possible prices for their trades.

The Power of Solana Blockchain
